BEFORE YOU HIRE

Verify the details.
Build with clarity.

Use this checklist before choosing any construction or real-estate provider.

  1. 01

    Team credentials

    Request verified registrations, relevant qualifications, and accountable project contacts.

  2. 02

    Property authority

    Confirm ownership, listing authorization, boundaries, and current availability.

  3. 03

    Material specifications

    Put brands, grades, quantities, substitutions, and quality checks in writing.

  4. 04

    Written pricing

    Ask for a detailed scope, payment milestones, exclusions, and change-order process.

  5. 05

    Delivery programme

    Agree on milestones, reporting, handover requirements, and delay responsibilities.

  6. 06

    Local requirements

    Confirm permits, approvals, utilities, insurance, and applicable Somaliland requirements.
